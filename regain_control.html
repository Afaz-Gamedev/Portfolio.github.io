[ Regain Control ]
Regain Control, a game similar to Naval Warfare but in space. The game consist of 2 teams each with 20 teamates on both sides. The objective of the game is to defeat the enemy by destroying the opposing cruiser which is used to deploy ships and manage the team's resources and spawnpoint. The game by itself is however never open to public due to the demanding resources and security outbreaks flawing the game but the legacy of the assets produced by me and some developers contributed to the development of the game lives on
